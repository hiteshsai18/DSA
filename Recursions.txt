Recursions :- A function solving a problem by breaking it into smaller problems.
> Two important components in recursion.
> 1. Base case - breaking case of our function (or) stopping condition / prevents infinite calls. 
> 2. Recursive case - function calls itself with smaller or larger input.

 Types of functions :
 1. Direct recursion
 2. Indirect recursion
 3. Head recursion
 4. Tail recursion
 5. Tree recursion
 6. Nested recursion
 7. Linear recursion
